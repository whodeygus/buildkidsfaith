Common Questions

Can I share the guides with my team?

Absolutely. One subscription covers your entire local church campus. You are free to print and distribute the Teacher and Volunteer guides to your staff.

Which Bible version is used?

The curriculum is designed to be version-agnostic, though primary scripture references are provided in clear, modern translations that are easy for children to grasp.

How is the content delivered?

Upon successful payment through Stripe, you will receive an email with a secure link to download your digital curriculum folders instantly.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. We are here to serve your ministry, not trap you in a contract. You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time through your billing portal.
